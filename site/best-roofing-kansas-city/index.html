What We Do

Roofing Done Right

Roof Replacement

When your roof has reached the end of its life or sustained severe storm damage, we handle the full replacement from tear-off to final inspection. We work with all major shingle brands and ensure every install is built to last Kansas City's toughest weather.

Storm & Hail Damage Repair

Hail and high winds can compromise your roof, gutters, windows, and more — and we know how to document it all for your insurance claim. Our team coordinates directly with your insurance company so nothing gets left off your settlement.

Roof Leak Repair

A leaking roof won't fix itself, and waiting only makes it worse. We thoroughly inspect the entire roof, pinpoint the source, and provide a clear quote before any work begins so you're never surprised.
