What We Do

Roofing Done Right

Roof Replacement

Whether your shingles are decades old or your decking has seen better days, we tear off the old and install a durable, beautiful new roof built to handle Kansas City weather. We work with asphalt shingles, tile, and more to match your home perfectly.

Roof Repair

From sagging sections and missing shingles to leaks and damaged flashing, our crew diagnoses the problem fast and fixes it right the first time. No patch jobs that fail in the next storm — just honest, lasting repairs.

Storm Damage Restoration

Hail and high winds can compromise your roof without you even knowing it. We inspect storm-damaged roofs, document the damage thoroughly, and work with your insurance company to get your home restored quickly and correctly.
